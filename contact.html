Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a property?

All our properties are available to book through Airbnb and Booking.com. Simply visit the property page and click on your preferred booking platform.

What is the minimum stay?

Minimum stay requirements vary by property and season. Most properties have a minimum stay of 2-3 nights, though we offer flexibility for longer bookings.

Is cleaning included?

Yes, all properties include professional cleaning before your arrival. For extended stays, we can arrange additional cleaning services at an extra cost.

Do you offer corporate rates?

Yes, we offer special rates for corporate bookings and long-term stays. Please contact us directly to discuss your requirements and receive a customized quote.
