Who We Are

Jumcent Properties is a leading provider of premium serviced accommodation across the United Kingdom. We specialize in offering comfortable, fully-furnished apartments designed to meet the needs of business travelers, corporate clients, and leisure guests.

Founded with a vision to transform the short-let accommodation experience, we combine professional property management with genuine hospitality to create spaces that truly feel like home.

Each property in our portfolio is carefully selected, professionally furnished, and maintained to the highest standards, ensuring every guest enjoys a comfortable and hassle-free stay.
